Siargao

Island life - S I A R G A O -

The island is within the jurisdiction of the province of Surigao del Norte and is composed of the municipalities of Burgos, Dapa, Del Carmen, General Luna, San Benito, Pilar, San Isidro, and Santa Monica.

Siargao is an island that is almost completely covered in coconut palm trees and surrounded by crystal-clear turquoise waters, making it a hard-to-beat tropical destination.

There are so many epic things to do in Siargao including white sand beaches, world-class surf breaks, unique cave pools, palm tree rope swings, island hopping, and much more!

My Gallery

Siargao Island is a year-round destination although the peak tourist season is in the dry season which is between the months of March and October. This is when there are likely to be fewer tourists.


For surfers visiting Siargao Island, the best months for surfing is during the swell season which is August through to November.

Visit

Outside these, the island remains largely untouched by tourism. Perfect for those that love kayaking, paddleboarding, cliff jumping, cave swimming, island hopping and plenty more besides with lots of eco-friendly accommodation.
